{{% ingredients %}}
- 1 cup pineapple, small diced
- 1/2 cup red onion, small diced
- 2 TBSP cilantro, roughly chopped
- 1/2 lime juiced
{{% /ingredients %}}

{{% instructions %}}
- Dice/chop fruit/veg
- Add lime and mix well
- Let sit to allow flavors to meld
{{% /instructions %}}
