{{% ingredients %}}
- 2 cans black beans
- 1 onion chopped
- 1 large tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- Lemon and lime juice to taste
- 2 tsp salt
{{% /ingredients %}}

{{% instructions %}}
- Do NOT drain beans
- Combine everything and simmer for 10-20 min on low heat
- Serves with slotted spoon
{{% /instructions %}}
