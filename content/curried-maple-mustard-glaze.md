{{% ingredients %}}
- 3 TBSP maple syrup
- 1 TBSP dijon mustard
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 TBSP butter
{{% /ingredients %}}

{{% instructions %}}
- Whisk maple, mustard, curry, and salt
- Melt butter in pan and add in above mixture cooking ~2 minutes mixing often until starts to bubble/thicken
{{% /instructions %}}
